Volunteer Language Support in Schools

For Schools

Classroom Support

For children with little or no English

EAL Volunteers work with schools to support children with little or no English, as well as provide opportunities for our volunteers to gain experience in an English speaking environment. Our network of volunteers are able to offer support with a wide range of languages, including Polish, Spanish, Arabic, Urdu and Chinese Mandarin. As an organisation run by teachers, we understand the challenges involved when supporting children who speak very little English. We offer advice and resources to help our volunteers work effectively with pupils, as well as helping them to feel engaged with their learning and to develop new friendships.

For Volunteers

Support and Development

For our volunteers, benefits may include taking opportunities to spend time working with members of their local community, practising language skills, developing confidence in a work environment, and learning new skills for future employment.
